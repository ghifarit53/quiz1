Banyuwangi,
Bumi Blambangan

Home to many traditional cultures, unique food, and popular tourist destinations ranging from mountains, forests, to beaches, Banyuwangi also served as the capital of the Blambangan Kingdom in the past

One particular food that I really love is called pecel rawon, and the most famous tourist destination here is a mountain called Kawah Ijen
